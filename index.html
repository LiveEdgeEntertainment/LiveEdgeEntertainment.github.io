What we do

Live Edge Entertainment is a live performance production company based in Las Vegas with a combined 60 years of experience producing shows and live events around the world.

Creation & Design

With backgrounds in musical theater, ballet, circus, magic, music festivals, special events, film and television, Live Edge partners have leveraged their performance experience into the creation and design of numerous successful productions including multiple Cirque Du Soleil shows, dinner-shows and charity galas. Our combined skill sets provide a single destination for writing, directing, choreography, costume design, lighting direction, scoring and act creation.

Production

Las Vegas is arguably the most competitive market in the world when it comes to shows and entertainment. With over 120 shows and concerts in town, running a successful production takes a team of local experts with knowledge of the specificities inherent to the Las Vegas market. With an extensive network of experienced directors, creators, producers, performers and relationships with all the major venues in the city, Live Edge Entertainment will help you navigate through the pre and post-productions process necessary to successfuly launch, market and operate your show.

Corporate & Private Events

Our Special Events Division brings world-class corporate and private experiences to life, crafting unforgettable moments for groups of all sizes. From intimate gatherings to large-scale productions, we specialize in immersive, cirque-style entertainment staged in extraordinary locations—including fully themed, decorated tents set directly on the iconic Las Vegas Strip. Live Edge Entertainment has produced special events for NASCAR, Formula 1 and the Super Bowl amongst many others. Every event is designed to transport guests into another world, blending breathtaking artistry, innovative staging, mouthwatering F&B and flawless hospitality to create experiences that linger long after the night ends.

Financing & Deal Structuring

With years of experience sourcing, financing and drafting entertainment deals, Live Edge Entertainment will help you find strategic partners and structure your production agreement. With robust intellectual property protection at the core of our approach, we help safeguard creative assets while enabling our clients to confidently move their ideas from concept to market.

    Laetitia Dewhurst

    Trained at the Royal Ballet School and English National Ballet School, Laetitia brings classical precision and contemporary edge to every stage she touches. She began her career as a principal dancer in acclaimed West End productions including Fosse and We Will Rock You, before joining Cirque du Soleil, where she performed in Zumanity. Transitioning seamlessly into choreography and direction, Laetitia has created and staged work for global brands and productions including Victoria's Secret, Crazy Horse, Celebrity Cruises, and major international festivals. Her work is known for its sculptural movement language, musical sensitivity, and refined theatrical polish. Together with Nicky, she has co-directed multiple editions of One Night for One Drop, including the 2018 edition featuring Grammy-nominated artist Jewel. Most recently, the duo served as creative writers and theatrical advisors for The Mayfair Supper Club at the Bellagio Las Vegas and continues to develop new work for Cirque du Soleil. Elegant yet daring, Laetitia’s work bridges ballet discipline and modern spectacle — always in service of emotion, story, and unforgettable imagery.

    Nick Dewhurst

    Born into one of the most storied families in modern circus, Nicky Dewhurst quite literally grew up under the big top. His father opened the first Cirque du Soleil show in Las Vegas alongside founder Guy Laliberté — a relationship Nicky himself has continued for decades as a close creative collaborator and friend. An internationally renowned tightrope artist and clown, Nicky joined five Cirque productions, including Mystère and Zumanity, where he created and originated the iconic clown character “Dick” and directed multiple comedy acts. His creative reach extends beyond the circus stage, contributing aerial choreography to Madonna’s Drowned World Tour and advising on performance for The Dictator. More recently, Nicky and his wife Laetitia served as creative directors for Guy Laliberté’s new entertainment venture, Frooogs, and has led large-scale creative direction for global events including Formula 1 and the Super Bowl. For over three decades, Nicky has fused daring physicality with razor-sharp theatrical instincts — creating spectacle that is bold, irreverent, and built to resonate at scale.

    Thomas Brunet

    Thomas is a finance executive and entertainment entrepreneur operating at the intersection of capital markets, corporate finance and large-scale live experiences. He is the co-founder of Live Edge Entertainment and founder of 1oak Investments, a boutique M&A and venture advisory firm focused on the gaming and entertainment industry. In that capacity, he advised on multiple transactions ranging from the sale of a top-tier sportsbook to raising Series A capital for a gaming technology company. He also co-produced “Inferno: The Fire Spectacular”, a large-scale magic show at the Paris Hotel in Las Vegas. Most recently, Thomas served as Founding Partner and CFO of Frooogs LV, the new entertainment venture owned by Guy Laliberté, founder of Cirque du Soleil. In that role, he led strategic finance, business development, and budgeting across multi-million-dollar productions, securing partnerships with top-tier casino operators and producing large-scale entertainment experiences tied to Formula 1, the Super Bowl, Coachella, NASCAR, and EDC. Earlier in his career, Thomas was a senior member of the FX Structuring group at BNP Paribas in London and designed systematic trading strategies within the Quantitative Strategy Group at Deutsche Bank in New York. He holds a Master’s in Financial Engineering from UC Berkeley and completed the Executive CFO Program at UC Berkeley. He is also a seed investor and board member in multiple entertainment and hospitality technology startups.
