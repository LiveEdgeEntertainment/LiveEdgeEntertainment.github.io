What we do

Live Edge Entertainment is a live performance production company based in Las Vegas with a combined 60 years of experience producing shows and live events around the world.

Creation & Design

With backgrounds in musical theater, ballet, circus, magic, film and television. Live Edge partners have leveraged their performance experience into the creation and design of numerous successful productions including multiple Cirque Du Soleil shows. Our combined skill sets provide a single destination for writing, directing, choreography, costume design, lighting direction, scoring and act creation.

Production

Las Vegas is arguably the most competitive market in the world when it comes to shows and entertainment. With over 120 shows and concerts in town, running a successful production takes a team of local experts with knowledge of the specificities inherent to the Las Vegas market. With an extensive network of experienced directors, creators, producers, performers and relationships with all the major venues in the city, Live Edge Entertainement will help you navigate through the pre and post-productions process necessary to successfuly launch, market and operate your show.

Technology

Our proprietary platforms allows for an enhanced customer experience and a seamless integration of technology into any live performance. From booking, concession ordering to interaction between the audience and the performers during the show, our state of the art app solves many of the inneficiencies of traditional thetrical productions.

    Nick Dewhurst

    Nicky comes from a performance background and has been involved in entertainment his whole life. At 12 years old, he performed a juggling act on the UK Television and went on to become an internationally renowned Tight Rope artist and Clown, performing all over the world. In 1994 Nicky founded Livewire Productions and has Since then staged and directed countless special events, with clients including American Express, Crysler, Canon, Avon, Abbott Labs, IBM, Victoria Secrets to name a few. Nicky was also part of the creation for Madonna’s “Drowned World Tour” aerial fight sequences and advised and performed on the Stunt team for Sasha Baron Cohens “The Dictator”. Nicky has been creating and performing for over 30 years, and has been part of 5 different Cirque Du Soleil productions including “Cirque Reinvente” “Nouvelle Experience” “Facination” “Mystere” and has created the role of “Dick” the clown in Cirque du Soleil’s “Zumanity” for which he is credited for the creation and direction of 3 comedy acts. Nicky Directed the show "Cosmopolitan" for Celebrity Cruises and Co directed with Laetitia Dewhurst, Cirque Du Soleil's "One Night for One Drop" 2017 at the Zumanity Theater Las Vegas and "One Night for One Drop" 2018 at the Michael Jackson Theater with Grammy nominated singer songwriter Jewel. More recently Nicky and Laetitia Collaborated as creative writers and theatrical advisers on the new "Mayfair Supper Club" at the Bellagio Las Vegas.

    Laetitia Dewhurst

    Laetitia Dewhurst has Choreographed and/or directed numerous events, including Victoria Secrets, Crazy Horse, Cirque Du Soleil, Curve Expo, Celebrity Cruise lines, Abbot Labs, Edinburgh Festival, GMC just to name a few. Laetitia trained at the Royal Ballet school, English National Ballet School and Performers Dance College Graduating with RAD & ISTD teaching diploma . Laetitia was original cast of Mystic Principal Dancer, Saturday Night Fever London, Fosse, We Will Rock you, Zumanity Cirque Du Soleil. She is currently still in Zumanity performing The Strap act, Pole act and understudy Female Clown.​ Laetitia Co directed with Nicky Dewhurst, Cirque Du Soleil's "One Night for One Drop" 2017 at the Zumanity Theater Las Vegas and "One Night for One Drop" 2018 at the Michael Jackson Theater with Grammy nominated singer songwriter Jewel. More recently Laetitia and Nicky collaborated as creative writers and theatrical advisers on the new "Mayfair Supper Club" at the Bellagio Las Vegas.

    Thomas Brunet

    Before founding 1oak Investments, Thomas was a senior member of the Foreign Exchange (FX) Structuring group at BNP Paribas in London. He priced and traded complex financial structures, providing cutting edge FX hedging solutions to large corporations and innovative FX investment strategies for institutional clients and hedge funds. Before joining the FX Structuring group, Thomas spent 5 years designing systematic trading strategies in the Quantitative Strategy Group at Deutsche Bank in New York. In this role he managed more than $1 billion of capital using statistical arbitrage models in the equity, FX, fixed income and commodity spaces. Bringing years of expertise from both the trading side and the investment management side of the financial markets, Thomas also has a Masters in Financial Engineering from the Haas School of Business at UC Berkeley and a Masters in Electrical Engineering from CPE Lyon, France.
